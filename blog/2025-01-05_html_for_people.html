Starting with HTML for People

So, I finally decided to do something with my SDF website. This started with an email from mom asking me for details about Tilly's graduation. Jennie mentioned that we should send out invitations, and I decided that it would be a good idea to make a website for her graduation announcement. I had recently seen a Mastodon post about the old-school beauty of hand-crafted websites, and linked to the HTML for People web-book. Since my skill writing HTML by hand is still pretty novice, I decided to follow along with the book setting up my site.

This is still rather rough. I plan to continue to update it in my free time over the next few weeks. The blog will journal my experiences setting up the site, as well as other random things I want to write about.
